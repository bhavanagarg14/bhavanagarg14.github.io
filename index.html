Transform Your Life with Science Based Nutrition

Bhavana Garg, founder of Bhavana Garg Nutrition and @bhavanagargofficial, is a certified nutritionist and holistic health coach. Bhavana is committed to offering a personalised and custom fit approach to health, lifestyle change, and wellbeing.

Bhavana helps her clients grow and thrive, unrooting the root cause of their health issues and helping them live healthier and happier lives.

Her passion began through her journey of learning how to care for her body and health. Bhavana's curiosity grew for both what was on her plate and what was going on inside her body. Using her knowledge for both nutrition, cooking food, and human physiology she is able to achieve incredible results in her practices with her clients.

Bhavana provides a full 360 approach to health and wellness, using nutrient analysis, deep understanding of food, and a natural touch. Creating awareness for your unique nutritional needs is the foundation in Bhavana's method. The process Bhavana uses is unique for each client as the way we respond to the foods we eat varies.

Work with Bhavana to lose weight, repair and restore your gut, balance your hormones, blood sugar and optimize your overall health. Find a way of eating and living that will transform your health and allow you to develop into the best and healthiest version of yourself.
