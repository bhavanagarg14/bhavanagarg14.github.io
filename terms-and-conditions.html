Terms of the Diet and Health Programs
  1. Program Inclusions
    The program fee includes:
    • One consultation call with Ms. Bhavana Garg
    • A personalised diet plan
    • Ongoing support via WhatsApp chat only for the duration of the program.
    Additional calls, if any, will be at the sole discretion of Ms. Bhavana Garg.
  2. Communication Protocol
    All client communication must be initiated via WhatsApp text or voice messages only. Please allow time for a response; messages will be answered as soon as possible. To avoid last-minute stress or confusion, clients are advised to plan their meals 2-3 days in advance.
  3. Response Time and Availability
    Ms. Bhavana often travels for work and may not respond immediately. However, she is committed to replying at the earliest possible opportunity.
  4. Client Responsibilities
    Daily meal photos and weight updates are mandatory. These are essential for monitoring progress and making informed recommendations. The effectiveness of the program depends significantly on the client's honest adherence to the prescribed plan and timely updates.
  5. Support Policy
    All support is strictly limited to WhatsApp chat and voice messages. Random or unscheduled phone/video calls are not permitted. Please message or voice-message your concern first and wait for a response.
  6. Response Time for Non-Plan Queries
    Questions not covered under your current diet plan (e.g., “Can I eat this?”) may take longer to be addressed.
  7. Refund and Cancellation Policy
    Diet plans are digital products and are non-refundable once delivered. You may take a break from your plan if needed, but this does not qualify for a refund or program extension unless otherwise specified.
  8. No Plan Revisions After Finalization
    Once your personalized diet plan is shared, no edits or additions will be made. Requests for extra recipes or dishes not originally included will be considered add-ons and communicated separately via WhatsApp or email.
  9. Weekend Policy
    Weekends (Saturday & Sunday) are designated as non-working days for Ms. Bhavana. Sundays are a complete day off for Bhavana Garg Health LLP. Messages received on Sundays will be addressed on the next working day.
